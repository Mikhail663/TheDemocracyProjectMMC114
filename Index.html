What is The Democracy Projects

The Democracy Project is a tech based initiative to create applications and social media alternatives focused on creating directly democratic organization on a large scale from the ground up. Imagine a world where the technology of algorithms and social media were not used to simply hold attention and sell products, but were utilized to rank posts utilizing metrics such as importance, time sensitivity, user skills and harm reduction priorities.

What if algorithms were used to link users with people across the world to coordinate solutions for the ever increasing world scale problems faced by today's global citizens. Sign up now to become involved in the start up team for this open source non profit project.
