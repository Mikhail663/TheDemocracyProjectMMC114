Posted on September 27, 2024, 2:00 PM by Mikhail

Introducing the start of The Democracy Project blog. Come here for all the latest impplematations of Direct Democracy into techonology, including updates on the develpment and progress of "The Democracy Project". We have some exciting plans that are only just beginning, we want to develop a strong community of web developers and coders that care about the furture of techonology and democracy. Currently in development are the community guidelines that will functionally serve as the constituion of "The Democracy Project" and in partiicaption in deveoping this project. We want to has as a diverse and inclusive range of participants as possible and thus will be solving the paradox of tolerance through the understanding of tolerance as a social contract. If you don't abide by the social contract you are not covered by it. What does this mean? It essentionally means that if you spread intolerance to people based of, sex, age, race, identity, disability, sexual orientation, etc... we are not obligated to tolerate your participation. This topic can become complex so stay tuned for updates.
